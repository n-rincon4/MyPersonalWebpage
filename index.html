My work

These are some of the projects I have worked on throughout my academic career:

Construction of an FTP and TCP-IP network client

Developed in Python, it transfers files between a domain and a host through the FTP and TCP-IP Protocols for stream sockets.
Worked with: Marilyn Joven & Valentina Reyes

Programming of a digital LED matrix

Developed in C and Verilog using Intel's STM32CubeIDE, it programs an LED matrix of size 7x8 so that a block of four points (2x2 grid) moves from top right to bottom left, row by row.
(In Spanish) Check out the video I made for this :)

Design of a filter for analog electrocardiogram noise

Design, simulation and implementation of a second-order Butterworth analog filter with Sallen-Key topology, used to cancel breathing noise in electrocardiogram readings.

Testing of a Multiband Monopole Antenna

Simulated in the Ansys HFFS software and physically proved at Los Andes University's anechoic chamber, based on Liu (2005).
Worked with: Francisco Santamaría & Juan Pablo Pacheco

Design of an automous coctail machine with Bluetooth

Based on analog VLSI design principles, it can prepare coctails by mixing up to three different drinks, controlled by a mobile app.
Worked with: Sebastián Guerrero, María Reales & Santiago Sinisterra

Design of a soccer fantasy team app

Developed in Eclipse IDE using Java, it is composed of a GUI made with the framework Swing and a user login feature.
Worked with: Gabriel Dicelis & Carolina Chinchilla
